Coupon - System


Your Perfect place to manage Coupons

Admin

The great Admin can manage Companies & Customers:


  • Create new Company/Customer & add it to the System

  • Update exist Company/Customer & add its new values to the System

  • Remove exist Company/Customer from the System

  • Get all Companies/Customers details

Company

The orginized Company can manage Coupons:


  • Create new Coupon & add it to the System

  • Update exist Coupon & add its new values to the System

  • Remove exist Coupon from the System

  • Get all its Coupons details

Customer

The simple Customer can manage his Coupons:


  • Purchase new Coupon & add it to the System

  • Get all of his purchased Coupons

  • Get all of his purchased Coupons by categories
